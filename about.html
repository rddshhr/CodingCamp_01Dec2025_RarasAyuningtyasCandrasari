Who We Are

Konnect is an Indonesia-based local travelling company dedicated to providing authentic local experiences to travelers. Our mission is to connect tourists with the rich culture, traditions, and hidden gems of Indonesia through personalized travel experiences.

Welcome to Konnect, where every journey tells a story.

Our Goals

Vision

To be the leading local travel company in Indonesia, renowned for delivering unforgettable and immersive travel experiences that celebrate the diversity and beauty of our country.

Mission

  • Provide safe, comfortable, and personalized travel services tailored to every traveler’s needs.
  • Support local communities by promoting local destinations, culture, and small businesses throughout Indonesia.
  • Deliver accurate, accessible, and user-friendly travel information through the Konnect digital platform.
  • Build strong connections between travelers and local guides to create closer, more authentic travel experiences.
  • Promote sustainable tourism by encouraging responsible travel that respects Indonesia’s nature and cultural heritage.
